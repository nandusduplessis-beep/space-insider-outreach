What This Is

This is your manual outreach command centre for the PNT Quantum Sensing report campaign. Every message is pre-written in Space Insider's brand voice. Your job: personalise and send.

Once HubSpot automation is live, these sequences will run automatically. Until then, this is the playbook.

Step-by-Step

1
Pick your ICP — Click one of the three target segments in the left panel. The full outreach sequence loads on the right.
2
Read the pain point — The header shows what problem this ICP has. Use it to frame your mindset before writing.
3
Follow the timeline — Each step shows the day, channel (LinkedIn or Email), and the exact message. Follow the cadence day by day.
4
Personalise the yellow brackets — Every [placeholder] must be replaced with real prospect data before sending. Check their LinkedIn profile, company news, recent announcements.
5
Hit Copy Message — Copies the full message to your clipboard. Paste into LinkedIn or your email client.
6
Track in HubSpot — Log the activity on the contact record. Even manual, we need the data for when automation takes over.

Response Handling

They SayYou Do
Positive replyBook a call within 1 hour. Send calendar link.
"Send more info"Reply with one specific insight from the platform. Propose 15-min call.
"Not the right time"Acknowledge. Offer no-commitment briefing on one PNT trend. Keep in nurture.
"We already have tools"Differentiate on advisory layer + graph mapping + mission data.
"Too expensive"Reframe around cost of missed opportunities. Offer focused pilot.
No responseContinue sequence. Never mention they didn't reply.

Tone — Do vs Don't

DoLead with insight, not features. Be specific (one data point beats three claims). Use lowercase subject lines. Say "worth a conversation?"
Don't"Just checking in." "Hope this finds you well." Exclamation marks. Feature lists. "We're the leading..." False urgency.
